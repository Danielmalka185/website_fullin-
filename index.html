How do I rent a charger?
Scan the QR code on any FULLIN station and pay instantly with your card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay. The charger will unlock automatically.
Do I need to download the app?
No. You can rent directly by scanning the QR code. However, the FULLIN app helps you find nearby stations and manage your rentals.
Where can I return the charger?
You can return the power bank at any FULLIN station in the network.
How much does it cost?
The price is ₪12 per hour with a daily maximum of ₪50.
What happens if I don’t return the charger?
If the charger is not returned within 48 hours, a ₪100 replacement fee will be charged
